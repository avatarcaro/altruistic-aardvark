An Ode To Two of My Favorite Things

I'm here to celebrate two simple objects that make my life significantly better.






I love pens.
I'm left-handed, so I am always looking for pens that don't smear when I write.
I also write really small, so I like finding pens that are 0.5mm or less to use most of the time.
I've found a few pens that I have used for years now: the Uni-Ball 207 series, micro point,
and the Pilot G2 Pens with an ultra fine point.
I appreciate good pens so much that I carry them with me almost everywhere so I don't have to use anything else.
I might take pens a little too seriously, but I'm really excited that I found ones I love.







If I ever get a chance to go to a store with a candle aisle,
there's a good chance I won't leave that aisle for at least an hour.
I love candles; I have especially come to love Homesick Candles that are designed to smell like specific places.
Homesick Candles has a candle scent for every state, and I just want to buy myself one of their candles to reminisce about every place I've lived.
Candles are really good at bringing up memories, and I love how smells have that kind of power.
